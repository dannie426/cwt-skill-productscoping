# Product Scoping Skill

A Claude skill for teams who want to take a product idea and turn it into something they can actually build — fast.

Built for hackathons and time-constrained build sessions, but useful any time you need to go from fuzzy idea to a clear, scoped direction.

---

## What it does

Runs a two-phase product scoping session:

**Phase 1 — Discussion**
Claude presents a structured set of questions covering your user, the problem, your unique angle, the experience you want to create, and your scope. Your team discusses them together and returns the answers — typed directly or as a transcript from a recording tool.

**Phase 2 — Extraction**
Claude takes your answers and produces two handoff documents:
- A **design handoff** covering the user, the feeling, the key moments, the core flow, and inspiration references
- A **build handoff** covering the idea statement, prioritised feature list, demo flow, what's out of scope, and any technical dependencies

Gaps are flagged honestly. Nothing is invented.

---

## How to use it

### Option 1 — Paste into Claude directly
1. Open [SKILL.md](./SKILL.md)
2. Copy the full contents
3. Paste into Claude and hit send
4. Claude will present the discussion questions
5. Work through them as a team, then paste your answers or transcript back in
6. Claude produces your two handoffs

### Option 2 — Use with a recording tool
1. Start a Granola session (or any transcript tool)
2. Use the question list in SKILL.md as your discussion guide
3. Export the transcript when done
4. Paste the transcript into Claude with the extraction prompt from SKILL.md

---

## Tips

- You don't need to answer every question — go deep on what matters most for your idea
- The more specific your answers, the sharper the handoffs
- The design handoff feeds directly into your designer or design tool
- The build handoff can be used as the starting point for your PRD or dropped straight into a coding agent like Claude Code or v0
